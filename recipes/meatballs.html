Meatballs

Description

This is a recipe that we have modified into our own. We first had it with HelloFresh, and then my sister-in-law made it for us with roasted carrots.

The secret to this recipe is the aioli that goes on the meatballs. It adds some heat, some creaminess, and an extra oomph of flavor.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • soy sauce
  • ground ginger
  • panko breadcrumbs
  • seasonings (onion powder, garlic powder)
  • steamed rice
  • green onion (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, soy sauce, ginger, breadcrumbs, and seasonings.
  3. Once all the ingredients are fully incorporated, section into evenly sized meatballs.
  4. Place meatballs on a lined baking sheet (for easy clean-up) and put in the oven for twenty minutes.
  5. Make the sauce.
  6. If using green onion as garnish, this is where I like to take the time to cut it.
  7. Rinse one cup of rice (serving size is 1/2 cup per person) and place in InstantPot with 1 1/4 cups of water.
  8. On high pressure, cook the rice for 5 minutes, letting it sit for five more before releasing pressure.
  9. Once the meatballs are done, dish rice into bowls, top with the meatballs, sauce, and green onion.
