Salmon

Description

A savory, quick, and easy recipe with few ingredients. It goes well with steamed rice and sriracha aioli.

The trick to this flaked salmon is to cook it slowly, and not force the flake. Then, once it's fully cooked & flaked, let it simmer while the sauce cooks down.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz flank of salmon
  • seasonings (powdered onion, powdered garlic, ground ginger)
  • soy sauce
  • maple syrup
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a skillet on medium-high.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add in the salmon and turn the heat to medium.
  3. Add all of the seasonings to the salmon. Let the salmon cook on one side for 3-4 minutes.
  4. While the salmon is cooking, add the soy sauce, maple syrup, and sesame seeds to a small bowl.
  5. Turn the salmon over, and season that side as well.
  6. After another 3-4 minutes, lightly scrape a spatula against the salmon. If it flakes easily, and is cooked all the way through, turn the heat to low.
  7. Add the sauce to the salmon, stirring so that each piece of salmon is covered in sauce.
  8. Let the salmon sit in the sauce for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Add the salmon to steamed rice, vermicilli noodles, or anything else you think it would be paired well with.
