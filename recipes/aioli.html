Aioli

Description

This is a tasty sauce that we use on multiple protein and rice bases, like in my meatball recipe. It can be made with homemade mayonnaise or greek yoghurt.

I like a creamier, softer taste than my husband (who came up with this sauce). I tend to use mayonnaise while he uses his greek yoghurt.

Ingredients

  • homemade mayonnaise/greek yoghurt
  • sriracha
  • soy sauce
  • honey

Steps

  1. I find it easiest to add the thickest ingredients first. In a small bowl, add the mayonnaise and honey.
  2. Add soy sauce next.
  3. Add sriracha to taste. This is personal preference.
  4. Using a whisk or fork, stir the ingredients until the aioli is fully mixed. Try the result, and add more of any ingredient to get the perfect taste.
